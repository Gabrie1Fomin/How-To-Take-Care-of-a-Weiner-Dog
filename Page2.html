Giving lots of Chewies

Giving your Dachshund chewies is important due to its ability to up your "cool points" with your dog.
If your dog does not recieve a daily chewy, he will become frustrated and will try to voice his concerns.
For my dog, the daily chewy marks the moment in which I leave for classes. I like to consider it a farewell gift.
He considers it as a peace offering in exchange for not destroying my room while I'm gone.
