Listening to your Dachshund

This one is the hardest. Because these little rascals cannot speak, they communicate via yips and yaps.
Occasionaly, you may be met with some pounces in which they express their utmost concerns with a silly and cute protest.
It is important to make sure your dog feels seen. If you laugh at their protest, they will continue to do so.
Take them seriously. They have a lot on their scheduele and getting laughed at isn't one of them.
Garfunkle is adamant on having one of my hats. Because of this, I have given him one of my favorite hats and watched him mangle it to shreds.
Now, people may say "don't give your dogs belongings to chew!" But how could I when Garfunkle asked nicely and he treats his toys with respect?
He even has his own pair of Crocs he's allowed to chew!
