Listening to your Dachshund

This one is the hardest. Because these little rascals cannot speak,
they communicate via yips and yaps. Occasionaly, you may be met with some
pounces in which they shal express their utmost concerns with a silly and cute protest.
It is important to make sure your dog feels seen. If you laugh at their protest, they will continue.
Take them seriously. They have a lot on their scheduele and getting laughed at isn't one of them.
